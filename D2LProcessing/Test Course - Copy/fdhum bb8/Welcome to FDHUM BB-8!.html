Welcome to FDHUM BB-8: History of the Jedi. This course is foundational for Padawans as it provides historical and philosophical study of the Jedi. Although this class is directed toward training Padawans, anyone may take it to gain a better understanding of the Jedi faith. Whether you are force-sensitive or not, by the end of this course you will be able to understand the historical importance of the Jedi and their role in galactic history.  

Be sure to read the course Syllabus and Schedule so that you understand what is expected of you as a student.
