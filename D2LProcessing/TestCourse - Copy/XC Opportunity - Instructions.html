Vampires are among the few Dark creatures with whom muggles have come into frequent contact. The esteemed professor, Gilderoy Lockheart, has written some highly credible information about vampires here, read it thoroughly. 

Over the years, muggle historians have recorded numerous accounts of vampire encounters, many of which are riddled with errors and obvious flaws. Some accounts, however, have proven to be quite educational and of scholarly merit to the wizarding world. One such account is the documentary of a muggle-raised half-blood, Buffy Summers.
