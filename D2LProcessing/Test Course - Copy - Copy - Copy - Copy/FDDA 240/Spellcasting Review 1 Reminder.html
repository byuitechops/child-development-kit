Spellcasting Review 1- Reminder

As the first quarter of our course comes to a conclusion it is time for a Spellcasting Review. Please schedule a meeting with your professor by owl. Your professor will apparate to you at the scheduled time and conduct the review. For this review, be prepared to perform the following spells and charms:

  • Rackablam
  • Lumos Solem
  • Duro
  • Bubble-Head Charm
  • Protego Horribilis
In addition to the above spells, be prepared to transfigure the following:
  • Water to ice
  • Dirt into garlic (reminder - never eat transfigured food)
  • Change shape of raw material (for example turn a wooden ball into a wooden stake)
Your Spellcasting review is due the Saturday of Lesson 3. Remember to prepare accordingly, and that if you do not schedule a time with your professor you will be given a zero on the assignment.
