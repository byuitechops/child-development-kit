Group Report

With your discussion group, write a report that synthesizes the material of Weeks 1 - 4.

Your report should include the following:

  • A brief overview of the Jedi Order
  • The difference between a Force-Sensitive and a Jedi
  • Comparisons of the light and dark sides of the force
  • An overview of lightsabers and their cultural significance to the Jedi

You will be graded on the following:

Content Report synthesized the concepts taught in Unit 1 and drew interesting conclusions to the discussion questions. (30 pts) Report covered one or two of the topics from Unit 1 and adequately explained their relevance in today's world. (15 pts) The report did not cover the topics from Unit 1 or shows a general misunderstanding of the principles taught in the Unit. (0 pts)
Spelling and Grammar The report was free of grammatical or spelling errors. (10 pts) Report contained one or two grammatical or spelling errors. (5 pts) Report reads like it was written by a scruffy Nerf Herder. (0 pts)
Submitted on Time Assignment was submitted on time for full credit. (10 pts) Assignment was submitted up to 48 hours late or previous arrangements were made with instructor for late work. (5 pts) Assignment was submitted late without appropriate communication or arrangements made. (0 pts)
